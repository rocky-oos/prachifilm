About Us

We are a full service studio, specialising in Wedding photography. We have a dedicated team of individuals who are skilled in different kinds of photography and can cater to specific needs of the client. The chief director/founder, who has completed his film studies from New York Film Academy in New York, has personally handpicked a highly efficient team of freelancers who assist him in his video&photo sojourns. The team prides itself on simple and aesthetic use of visuals to convey a story. We prefer not to complicate things by overly manipulating photos & videos.The beauty of an image lies in its simplicity. Our motto has always been to provide an honest, pleasant experience to our customer and to convert their input into palpable output.
